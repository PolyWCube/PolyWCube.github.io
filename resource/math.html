MATH

The study of mathematical objects, logics, patterns, structures and their relations, which provide a new way of thinking as well as understanding and predicting surrounding world.


Topic

    The backbone of arguments, providing a structured way to connect premises to conclusions in reasoning.

    Provides a framework to create connection between related arguments to express logic and the proposition of a statement.

  • MEASUREMENT SYSTEM

    Provides a objective, standard way to understand the unit of an object or abstraction.

    Learn to convert between measurement units.

  • FUNCTION

    Present a more abstract way to express an result depend on the parameter.

  • STATISTIC

    Study about the fundamental of understanding the large dataset.

    Learn to read dataset in a more effective way and make more accurate predictions.

  • NUMBER

    The way to express quantity of objects as well as provide a framework on understand the concept of other mathematical objects.

    Fundamental basis of math, essential for formulating relationship between argument.

  • OPERATION

    The way to express relation between objects as well as provide a framework on understand the concept of other mathematical objects.

    Deal with weight, node value and abstract distance.

  • VECTOR

    The abstractional method to show the direction of a value.

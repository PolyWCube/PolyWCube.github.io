Protein

The a single or multiple polypeptide chain with the complete and stable conformation.


Here is 2 primary nucleic acids:

  • DNA

    The most well-known of all nucleic acid, store and transmit genetic information of most organisms

  • RNA

    Primary occurs for the gene expression process, but still the exist as genetic storage or transmiter.

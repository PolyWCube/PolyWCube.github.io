Level Of Organization

Levels of biological organization refer to the hierarchy of levels organized organism.

They are arranged from the simplest to the most complex

  • Atom
  • Cell

    The base of every higher level of organisation, create the structure and function of them.

  • Tissue
  • Organ
  • Organ System
  • Body
  • Organism
  • Group
  • Population
  • Ecosystem
  • Biosphere

which can be divide into 2 catagories:

  • Fundamental level

  • Intermediate level

Property

Integrative: The set of phenomena arise from the complex interactions of components at lower levels of organization create emergent property.

Genetic Mechanism

At monocule level

  • Gene Expression

    The biological process of genetic information from gene create a functional product which affect a phenotype of organism. In most case, gene expression follow the path of DNA - RNA - protein - phenotype.

  • Gene Regulation

    The mechanism allows living organisms to save and use energy and matter efficient within their life cycles, making them more capable for life.

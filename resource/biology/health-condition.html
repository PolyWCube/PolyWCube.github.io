Health Condition

Any health state that deviates from normal functioning, encompasses a wide range of issues in both physical and mental health problem.

Disease

The specific conditions with identifiable causes, signs, and symptoms.

Disorder

The functional abnormalities that with unclear and unidentificable causes, signs, and symptoms.

Genetic Disorder

The disorder caused by one or more abnormalities in genome, include mutation in a single gene (monogenic) or multiple genes (polygenic) or chromosome.

Syndrome

The collection or set of causes, signs, and symptoms that tend to occur together and characterise a particular condition.

Other Health Condition

The health condition causes by external force injuries, infections, virus, radiation exposed.


  • Human Immunodeficiency (HIV) - Disease
  • Openheimer - Disease
  • Alzheimer - Disease
  • Influenza (Flu) - Disease
  • Phenylketonuria (PKU), Phenylalanine Hydroxylase Deficiency - Disease
  • Malaria - Disease
  • Down - Disease

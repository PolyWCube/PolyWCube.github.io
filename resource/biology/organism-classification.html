Organism Classification

The sciencific name classification of organism base on it's shared characteristic and structure. Organisms are grouped into distincted catagories, rank and intergrated to form a more includesive group of higher rank which creating a hierarchy reflect the relation or differentiateion between organisms. For instance, Homo Sapien is the classification name of the mankind or the modern human, is the last suriving species of genus Homo, of Mammal domain.

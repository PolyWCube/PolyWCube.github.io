Biology

The study of life and everything related to it.

This is a branch of natural science study about lifeform (living organism), characteristic of life and it's behaviour, to revealing the fundamental and secret of life.

Topic

  • CHARACTERISTIC OF LIFE

    The definition of life, the distinct different about living and non-living thing.

    The characteristic allow organism to stablizing it internal structure, living and adapting to environment.

  • ORGANISATION LEVEL

    Life create from multiple level of biological structure.

    The level of biological organization represent the layer of life, follow the concept of higher, more complex structure made up of smaller, simpler structure.

  • VITAL PROCESS

    For life to even exist in the first place, vital process have a essential in the manipulation of both energy and matter.

  • CALCULATION

    The meaning and role behide numbers and calculations depend on the structure and function of a micromolecule.

    The surrounding space with conditions and resources for life to exist.

  • EVOLUTION
